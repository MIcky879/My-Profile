Project Idea

Overview

Design and development of an multi player shooter game in a battle royal setting, themed around RMIT where the players are students aiming to hand in an assignment. The game will be a timed battle, in which a group of players will enter as teams and try to survive to the end. The arena would be based around the CBD, and to prevent players from being immobile, a ring called the “Deadline” will periodically shrink and deal dmg to the players, if they are touched by it. The goal of the game would be, to be the last surviving team at the end of the game, this would be achieved by other being the last team surviving at the last ring or by eliminating the other players.

Motivation

This project is designed by a student, for students. Games are designed to be a fun enjoyable experience aimed to help whoever plays them destress and enjoy themselves. The project is to be themed around the RMIT university and the CBD and parodied to seem comedic. This is in hope any RMIT student that plays this game would gain some cathartic feelings from digitally shooting other students and destress themselves from the possible emotional turmoil gained from the workload of their studies. The projects end goal is that, for whoever that plays this has fun and enjoys playing the project.

Disclaimer, the project, and team does not in any way condone the act of shooting of students,murder,taking of life,theft, etc.

Description

These are Key functions needed in this game:

The player: must be able to move in all directions in 360 degrees, change the speed of movement to sprint and back to walk to suit the player, interact with the environment or items, pick up items, aim in the direction of shooting the firearm, shoot and reload said firearm, have a health point pool which can be damaged and when reduced to zero will exit the game.

The ring: periodically closes in at fixed intervals, pausing to give players time to re-enter the ring or to re locate within it. Also, it would deal damage to the player’s health point pool, if the player is outside of it, this damage would scale in intensity given how many rings have passed within the game.

The map: should direct players to the centre of the ring as it is the main goal of the game, a mini map would accompany the player for quality-of-life map viewing. A full map would be possible to be viewed and closed at a button press. Landmarks on the map would be outlined, stating if the loot in the area would be high quality or not.

Loot Boxes: need to be interactable, able to provide players with possible loot, namely, a gun, armour, ammo, healing items, these would be provided at a random rate and varying in quality based off its location, randomized each game.

Guns: to be pick up able and would be categorised by type, differentiating from each other via effective range, magazine size, damage per pellet, and accuracy per shoot.

Armour: to be pick up able and will act as an additional health point pool in which can be used to shield the players normal health point pool, and able to evolve though damage cause by the player, leveling up to 4 levels having a larger health pool at each level.

Classes: Specialised classes would be based off student programs, each with possible unique passives or active actions. Engineering students would increase the magazine size of the gun they currently have equipped. Medical student would passively heal their health pool at a slow periodic pace. Physical education students would have an increased movement speed, be it running or walking. Information technology students would have a specialised interaction in which, they would interact with a computer and the next location that ring would pause at, would be displayed on their map only.
